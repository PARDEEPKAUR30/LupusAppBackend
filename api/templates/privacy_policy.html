Privacy Policy for Lupus Check

Effective Date: 11-03-2015

At Lupus Check, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with the utmost care. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using the Lupus Check app (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with accurate assessments based on the 2019 EULAR/ACR classification criteria for Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE):

  • Clinical Symptoms: You will be asked to provide information about your clinical symptoms, including but not limited to, details about physical symptoms you may be experiencing.
  • Test Results: If applicable, you may provide medical test results, such as lab work or diagnostic tests relevant to SLE symptoms.

This information is solely used to assess and classify your health condition based on the validated criteria for SLE.

We do not collect or process any personal identifiers (such as your name, contact details, or address) unless required for a specific feature you choose to use.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used exclusively to assist in classifying your health condition based on the 2019 EULAR/ACR classification criteria for Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE):

  • Classification Based on Criteria: Your clinical symptoms and test results are analyzed according to the 2019 EULAR/ACR classification criteria to provide an assessment of your likelihood of having SLE.
  • Improvement of Services: We use anonymized data to improve the functionality and accuracy of the App.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the protection of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to safeguard the information you provide. While no method of data transmission or storage can be guaranteed as 100% secure, we follow industry best practices to ensure the security of your health-related data.

Data Retention: Your health-related data will be retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your health information to third parties. Your data is used solely within the scope of providing the services of Lupus Check.

We may share your information in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your health data (if applicable).
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by uninstalling the app or ceasing use of the service.

Please note that the app does not provide functionality for updating or deleting the data you have entered. If you have any concerns about the data you have provided, please contact us using the details below.

6. Children's Privacy

Lupus Check is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Significant changes will be communicated by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
